Welcome to Senior Seva Helpline

Senior Seva Helpline is a FREE service to the senior citizens of Belagavi city which helps with

  • On-boarding for Govt Schemes.
  • Home Delivery Services.
  • Healthcare connects through vendor tie-ups.

In addition, the Senior Seva Mitra establishes a young buddy tie-up program to provide Companionship & Accompaniment for Elders via

  • Hiring & training young adults & students to empathize with seniors.
  • Adopting age-tech tools to efficiently manage service requests.
  • Facilitating ease-of-use and serviceability via a simple phone call.
